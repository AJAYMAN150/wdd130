Hawaii, U.S.A.

Hawaii is a beautiful state smack dab in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. The beaches are nice, but if you want the best beaches, you'd need to take your sightseeing away from the cities and explore the rest of the islands. We welcome visitors with the spirit of Aloha and sunny smiles, so long as they respect our islands when visiting. It also rains quite a lot, but can differ depending on which part of the island you're on. We have two temples in Hawaii: the Kona temple on the biggest island, known as "The Big Island," and the Laie Temple in Oahu, where I live.
