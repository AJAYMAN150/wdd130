    Hawaii, U.S.A.

    Hawaii is a beautiful state smack dab in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. The beaches are nice but if you want the best beaches you'd need to take your sight seeing away from the cities and explore the rest of the islands. We welcome visitors with the spirit of Aloha and sunny smiles, so long as they respect our islands when visiting. It also rains quite alot but can differ depending on which part of the island you're on. We have two temples in Hawaii. The Kona temple in Hawaii, which is the name of the biggest island but everyone calls "The Big Island". The other being the Laie Temple in Oahu, the island of where I live.
