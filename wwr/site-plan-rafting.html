Rafting Site Plan

Xavier Rodriguez

Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to take my business and expand. I'm aiming for more potential customers, and that can be achieved by how well our website turns out. In order for that to happen, we need to get the word out. So we'll need ads, and the quickest way to have people see them is through social networking. We'll start small to get local attention. Once a steady ad revenue is in, the profit can be used to improve the site and continue reaching out for better ad slots. We'll go local, then state, and next will be the entire country.

Audience

The targeted audience are namely families and outdoor fanatics, preferably ages 13 and up. We can provide locations where customers will be able to raft safely, but things such as food and equipment must be brought by the customers themselves.
